What Are Ecobricks?

Definition

An Ecobrick is a clean, dry plastic bottle densely packed with non‑biodegradable plastic. When made properly, it becomes a durable, modular block for building.

Why it works

  • Reduces plastic leakage by safely containing plastic.
  • Raises awareness and changes daily habits.
  • Creates useful community assets from otherwise valueless waste.

Where to use Ecobricks

Common uses include garden planters, seating, low walls, raised beds, and as infill within frames for temporary structures. Combine with earth or cob for stability.
