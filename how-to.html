How‑To: Make Safe, Durable Ecobricks

  1. Collect & sort: Focus on soft plastics (wrappers, bags, films). Avoid organics or metal.
  2. Wash & dry: Clean thoroughly and dry completely to prevent mould.
  3. Choose a bottle: Same brand/size per project improves uniformity.
  4. Pack densely: Cut plastics into small pieces; compress with a stick. Aim for high density.
  5. Weigh & label: Record weight, date, and maker for quality tracking.
  6. Quality check: Brick should be rigid with no springy gaps or soft spots.

Safety & Ethics

Ecobricks are a transitional, community‑education tool—not a license for more plastic use. Prioritise reduction and reuse; use Ecobricks to manage unavoidable plastic while pushing for systemic change.
